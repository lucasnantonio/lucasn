Lucas Neumann

I’m a designer with a multiple background, always working to increase the quality of people’s experiences with the services, physical and digital products they encounter every day.

I have worked with branding at FutureBrand, research and innovation at Insitum, UX at Kano Computing, and I’m currently helping the Our Cities Network create digital products that foster civic engagement and democratic participation.

Some of my work got featured in Fast Company, Wired.com and PSFK.

I read a lot, am crazy about lists and organisation, plus love chatting about tech, psychology, economics, behaviour and public policies.
